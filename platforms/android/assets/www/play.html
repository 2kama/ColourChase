Colour-Chase is a game that aims at improving your brain's ability to recognise colors.
It has modes that are differentiated by the number of boxes to choose from when playing.


TIMER
Just tap the mini box that has the same color with the board box before the timer runs out. Every 20 consecutive taps you get correct reduces the time frame of the game hereby prompting you to go faster. Time frames are in the order 4s, 3s, 2s, 1s, 0.5s, 0.2s and so on (you so would be a speedster to reach the end).

SPEED
This aims at seeing how many correct boxes you can tap per second. You'd have to make 20 right taps in the shortest time possible.
